I've been using Claude for months. Thought I knew everything it could do.

Then I found Claude Skills. And my jaw dropped.

Here's what I didn't know — Claude has a plugin system.

🔌 **Plugin** — one install, and Claude gains an entirely new domain of capability
🧠 **Skills** — custom instructions that teach Claude exactly how to execute complex tasks
🤖 **Agents** — autonomous AI specialists that spin up, take ownership, and get things done

Think of it like hiring a new employee —
one command, and they're in. Already trained. Already specialised. Ready to work.

Stack enough of them and Claude stops being a chatbot.
It becomes a system.

Then one day, I went down a rabbit hole reading other people's plugins,
and a thought hit me:

*What if I built something actually powerful, by myself? Something, production-grade?*

I had no idea what I was getting myself into.

🧵 Part 1 of 5 — follow along. By Part 5, you'll get why I'm still geeking out.
