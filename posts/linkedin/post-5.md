# Post 5 - The One I Almost Didn't Build
**Series:** Building with Claude · Part 5 of 5
**Graphic:** assets/linkedin-post-5.png (1280×720)

---

I almost didn't build this one.

Voice AI felt too complex.
Assistant config. Phone numbers. Webhooks. Tools. Squads. Calls. Analytics.
I kept putting it off for weeks.

Then one day I stopped waiting and just started. One skill. Then another.

About two skills in, it hit me - I'd done this exact thing before.
Same pattern. Same framework. Completely different domain.

Three weeks later: **Vapi Super Creator**.

Not just a tool that creates voice agents - one that creates *production-ready* ones.
From first prompt to a live phone call. Every config validated. Every edge case handled.
Nothing ships broken.

That's when I stepped back and looked at what I'd actually built.

→ Airtable Super Creator - database automation, from schema to production
→ Canvas Design - AI that creates pixel-perfect visuals from a single prompt
→ Vapi Super Creator - voice AI, end to end, fully validated before it ships

Three completely different domains. One framework. One install away.

So I packaged all three into **Claude SuperKit** - open source, free to use, ready for your workflow.

This series started with me stumbling across a plugin system I didn't know existed.
It ends with me shipping one of my own.

⭐ github.com/pokharnajay/claude-superkit

And this is just the start - more plugins, skills, and agents are already in the works.

🧵 End of series - thanks for following along from Part 1.
